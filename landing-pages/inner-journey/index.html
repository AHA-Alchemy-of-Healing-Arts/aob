What Is the Inner Journey?

A 7-chapter course.
One honest look at yourself.

Most of us are running on autopilot. Patterns we picked up in childhood still shape how we love, work, argue, and retreat. We do not choose them. We just repeat them.

The Inner Journey is a video course that walks you through those patterns, one chapter at a time. It is part teaching, part reflection, part breathwork, and entirely at your own pace. By the end of seven weeks, you will have a much clearer map of the inner terrain that has been running your life.

It is not therapy. It is not a bootcamp. It is a quiet, structured way of meeting yourself on purpose.

Click any step above to open that chapter.

Each chapter is a video teaching with reflection prompts and a guided breathwork session.

01

Chapter One  ·  Week 1

Opening the Book of Your Life

Where your story actually begins. The pre-birth and early experiences that set the starting point you have been working from ever since. A chapter of orientation: looking at the map before you walk.

02

Chapter Two  ·  Week 2

The Call

The moment you stop waiting for someone else to fix it. What self-responsibility actually looks like, and the quiet invitation to change that starts with you, not with the people around you.

03

Chapter Three  ·  Week 3

The Inner Child

The younger version of you who is still calling the shots in the parts of your life that do not seem to listen to reason. Learning to hear them, and to meet them with something other than frustration.

04

Chapter Four  ·  Week 4

Embracing Your Shadows

The parts of you that you have been hiding, even from yourself. What gets triggered and why, and the surprising freedom on the other side of looking directly at what you have been avoiding.

05

Chapter Five  ·  Week 5

Finding the Treasure

What you have already done, already built, already become. The quiet road toward mastery does not start where you think it does. This chapter is a pause to acknowledge what is already here.

06

Chapter Six  ·  Week 6

Atonement and Return

The passage from knowing to not-knowing, from mastery to mystery. What you stop needing to control, and what opens up when you do. A softer kind of strength.

07

Chapter Seven  ·  Week 7

The Return

The ending that is also the beginning. A gentle passage through what must end, what wants to be born, and how to meet both with more presence than you are used to.

How It Works

A simple weekly rhythm.

The same shape every week. Different territory each time.

Watch

The chapter teaching. Around 30 to 45 minutes with Anthony.

2

Reflect

Journal prompts designed for that chapter. As little or as much as you like.

3

Breathe

A guided breathwork session matched to the chapter's theme.

4

Integrate

Let the week do its work. Come back when you are ready for the next chapter.
